    Welcome To Sunshine Kindergarten

    At Sunshine Kindergarten, We care about building our students basic knowledge in main subjects like english , math and science with fun activites that will prepare them for their outstanding future.

    Weekly Schedule:


    • Week 1: Students will be introduced to Reading Alphabets in English.

    • Week 2: Students will start Writing the English Alphabets, numbers and basic science with the help of their teacher.

    • Week 3: We will have volunteers from British Consuel visting our Kindergarden and providing fun activities for our students.

    • Week 4: Students will have in-class activities to test what they have learnt in the past weeks.
